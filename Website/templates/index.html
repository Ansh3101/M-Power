M-POWER

A web application that reads MRI scans and various other reports

Why US?

The doctor-patient ratio in our country is very low and fatal diseases like Cataract,Lung and Breast Cancer are curable in most cases, if detected early. The lack of a robust medical infrastructure and a good number of doctors who can suffice the need of people in the villages, costs many lives.With our project we plan on making diagnosis easy and accessible to all.
Our project allows the users to upload necessary medical scans and reports to detect various diseases without the need of a doctor. We do not aim to replace doctors but guide the patient and inform them about their current medical standing based on their reports and advise them on how urgent it is for them to consult a doctor .
We use medical reports and run them through various Machine Learning and Deep Learning models that we have built to classify and segment the reports. We make primary use of the Convolutional Neural Networks for the processing of images, YoloV5 and Detectron2 for image segmentation and Simpler Supervised Learning methods like Support Vector Machines, Random Forests and CatBoosting for things like heart disease detection
